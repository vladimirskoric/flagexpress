About Us

The Frontliner Logistics Assistance Gateway or FLAGexpress.ph is a system which allows individuals and organizations to donate goods for our front-liners in Metro Manila.

This is a project of the Office of Civil Defense (OCD) for a transparent system of donations for our front-liners. DCTx provided the digital assistance to realize this project. DCTx is a worldwide volunteer-based community of experts from various fields who are developing digital solutions to alleviate the struggle of Filipino front-liners against COVID-19.
